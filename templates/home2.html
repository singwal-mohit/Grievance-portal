Welcome to The Grievance Portal

Grievance Portal

Grievance Portal - The ultimate solution - is a multi-purpose online web enabled platform based on web technology which primarily aims to enable submission of grievances by the authorised members of the University from anywhere and anytime (24x7) basis to all the different departments of the University who scrutinize and take action for speedy and favorable redress of these grievances. Tracking grievances is also facilitated on this portal through the system generated unique status bar.
If you have not got a satifactory redress of your grievance within a reasonable period of time, the the issue will be displayed on the home page of the portal so as to highlight the issue.
